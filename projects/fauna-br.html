Fauna Brasil was incredible designed by Bruno Mezzomo, and I developed it with OpenCart3, and used shared hosting to share it.

How is the development process?

First, I received the wireframes, prototypes and source from Bruno. Then, I have instaled OpenCart3, and start coding the front-end. After the front-end, I installed the shipping and payment plugins, and created some to give freedom to the admin to change stuff on front-end without a code change.

What type of changes?

Just like the main banner photos, the highlights and the partners, on partners section. All of this is customized on the OpenCart dashboard.
